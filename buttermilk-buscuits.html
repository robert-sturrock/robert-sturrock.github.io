Buttermilk Buscuits

Ingredients

  • 230ml milk
  • 1tbsp white vinegar
  • 1.75 cups plain flour
  • 6tsp baking powder
  • 3/8tsp baking soda
  • 6tbsp butter

Steps

  • 1. Mix dry ingredients and butter in a few short bursts in a food processor. It should end up in small crumbs.
  • 2. Make a bowl in dry ingredients and add the milk vineargar which should have been pre mixed for 10 min. Mix well. Should be wet but doughy.
  • 3. Heavily flour countertop. Scoop dough out and heavily flour top. Kneed a bit. Then roll out to 1-1.5cm. Cut with 6-8cm circle cut out.
  • 4. Bake at 220c for 10-15 mins till top is golden
