Quick Moroccan Meatballs

Ingredients

  • 350g ready-made beef or chicken meatballs
  • 1bsp olive oil
  • 1 large onion
  • 100g dried apricot, sliced
  • 1 small cinnamon stick
  • 400g tin chopped tomato
  • 25g toasted flaked almonds
  • coriander, chopped

Steps

  • 1. Cook the meatballs in a pan until nearly done (about 10min). Remove from the pan.
  • 2. Add onions and cook for 5min with the cinnamon stick. Then add tomato, apricots, and half a can of water. Cook for 5-10min. Remove cinnamon stick and add meatballs.
  • 3. Scatter almonds and coriander on top. Serve with Couscous.
