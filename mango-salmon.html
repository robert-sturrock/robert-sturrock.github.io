What I like to make

Roast salmon with lime, mango, avocado salad

Ingredients

  • 2 medium avocados
  • 300g cherry tomatoes
  • 1 cucumber
  • 2 ripe mangos
  • 1 red chili (use half)
  • rocket/fresh basil or mint to garnish
  • 2 limes
  • 4 salmon filets
  • olive oil, salt & pepper

Steps

  • 1. Zest both limes. Top the salmon filets with 1 1/2 limes worth of zest and 1 lime of juice, salt, pepper, and a drizzle of olive oil. Put in oven at 180C (with fan) for 13min.
  • 2. Finely slice up mango, cucumber, tomatos, chili and mix together in a bowl. Add in remaining lime juice and zest, and tbsp of olive oil.
  • 3. Remove salmon from the oven and plate on top of the salad. Add rocket and herbs to garnish. Serves well with a nice bread.
