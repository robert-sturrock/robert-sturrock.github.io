What I like to make

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 200g pomegranate seeds/2 pomegranates
  • 2 small green apples
  • 200g baby spinach
  • 30g fresh mint/basil
  • 2 cups rice
  • 2 limes
  • olive oil
  • chili flakes
  • walnuts

Steps

  • 1. Heat the oven to 200C. Place the chicken breasts and avocado (halved and pitted) in the pan and coat lightly with olive oil, chili flakes, salt and pepper. Place in the oven for 25min until fully cooked.
  • 2. Bring water to a boil. Add in rice and cook for 20 minutes until slightly al dente.
  • 3. Remove chicken breasts and avocado from the pan. Add the rice to the roasting pan and mix with the juices. Slice up the baby spinach and add. Mix up the lime juice, 6tbps olive oil, chopped mint/basil, and pomegranate seeds as a dressing pour 3/4 over the pan. Mix.
  • 4. Slice up the chicken and add it and the avocado back to the pan. Scatter over walnuts and add remaining dressing.
