Chicken pot pie

Ingredients

  • 500g sweet potatoes
  • 1bsp olive oil
  • 1 large onion
  • 3 thyme sprigs (or more)
  • 2 garlic cloves (or more)
  • 350g chicken breasts, cut into chunks
  • 250 chestnut mushrooms
  • 300ml chicken stock
  • 100g creme fraiche (or coconut creme)
  • 100g kale
  • 2 tsp cornflour, mixed with 1tbsp cold water
  • 1 tbsp wholegrain mustard
  • 375g pack puff pastry

Steps

  • 1. Bring a pot to boil. Peel and cut sweet potato into rough 1 inch cubes. Boil until a fork will stick into the pieces with a slight push.
  • 2. (Simultaneously) Heat 1/2 tbsp oil over a gentle heat in a flameproof casserole dish. Add the onion and cook for 5 mins until softening. Scatter over the thyme and garlic, and stir for 1 min. Turn up the heat and add the chicken, frying until golden but not fully cooked. Add the mushrooms and the remaining oil. Heat oven to 200C/180 fan/gas 6.
  • 3. Add the stock, crème fraîche, mustard and kale, and season well. Add the cornflour mixture and stir until thickened a little (this mixture should be made earlier in the process for best results).
  • 4. Remove from the heat and cover with the puff pastry lid, pressing into the sides of the casserole dish. Slice a cross in the centre and glaze with the egg. Bake for 30 mins until the pastry is puffed up and golden.
