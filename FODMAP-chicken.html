What I like to make

FODMAP friendly pesto chicken

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 300g cherry or plum tomatoes
  • 3tbsp pesto
  • 3tbsp creme fraiche (oat based/coconut creme for dairy free)
  • salt & pepper
  • fresh basil
  • olive oil

Steps

  • 1. Pan fry chicken breast with olive oil, salt, and pepper until mostly done (155C).
  • 2. Add in sliced tomatoes, pesto, creme fraiche, and a bit of water to make sauce. Stir.
  • 3. Add chopped basil to top and serve.
